My Favourite Things

My name is Nsongurua. This web page lists my favorite foods, movies and music

My Favorite Foods

  1. Fried Rice

    This is rice that has been fried. The taste and aroma is on another level. it goes well with fried chicken and a cold drink.

  2. Pounded Yam

    Yam is peeled, sliced and cooked. It is thereafter pounded. It is mostly eaten with white soup.

  3. Yam and Beans

    This is beans cooked with yam.

My Favorite Movies

Arrow This is a series about a young man who was stranded on an island for 5 years. He eventually returns as a changed man; a billonaire play boy in the day and a vigilante at night. It's very interesting as it has a lot of tech in it.


Spiderman A high school student who was often bullied at school gets bitten by an enhanced spider at a research lab. This gives him super powers. This movie also has a lot of tech and detective actions.


The Transporter A man is contracted to deliver precious cargos on time. All goes well untill he encounters an unexpected cargo. This movie can increase your adrenaline rush.
